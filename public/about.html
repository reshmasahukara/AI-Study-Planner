Our Mission

Building the ultimate intelligent scheduling system to solve student burnout.

The Problem We're Solving

Modern students are expected to juggle university academics, Data Structures and Algorithms practice, competitive exams like GATE or CAT, and portfolio building—all at the same time. The cognitive load of simply planning what to study often leads to decision fatigue and burnout before the actual studying even begins.

The Solution

AI Study Planner was built to offload the cognitive burden of scheduling to an intelligent machine. You tell it what you need to learn, and our algorithm dynamically segments, prioritizes, and schedules your entire day down to the minute.

Core Values

🧠 Consistency over Intensity

We enforce smart breaks to ensure you can study for months without crashing.

⚡ Dynamic Agility

Life happens. When a task runs over by 30 minutes, the rest of the schedule shifts automatically.
